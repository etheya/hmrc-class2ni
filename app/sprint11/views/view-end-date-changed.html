What you need to know

Any gaps between periods may be because you:

  • were on Employment Support Allowance (sickness, invalidity or incapacity benefit)
  • claimed Maternity Allowance
  • stopped and re-started self-employment
  • weren't actively seeking self-employment
Information You still have to pay Class 2 National Insurance during a temporary break if you plan to continue with self-employed work.
What's a temporary break in self-employment?
  • times you're not carrying out self-employed work, but still actively seeking it and advertising for it
  • gaps between self-employed projects
  • taking holidays abroad or in the UK
  • writers undertaking research
  • jury service

If you think you have any missing periods of self-employment, you can add them by using the CWF1 form (opens in a new tab).
Additions may take up to 48 hours to appear.
